Welcome to Replate!

For those of us who are too busy to look up ways to reuse some leftover food, Replate lets you find solutions at the tip of a finger. It allows anyone, anywhere, to capture or upload a picture of food, scan it and get suggestions on what to do with the food, helping reduce food waste on our planet.

Why is food waste such a big deal?

Ever wondered what happens to those bits of leftover rice that you threw away last night? Now imagine seven billion people doing the same...
Each year, 1.3 billion tons of food waste worth hundred billions of dollars is produced, and it has a colossal impact on both the environment and economy!
